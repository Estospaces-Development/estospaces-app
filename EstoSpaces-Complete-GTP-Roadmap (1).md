## Risk Management

### Critical Risks

#### 1. **Broker Adoption Risk**

**Risk:** Brokers don't sign up or use the platform

**Likelihood:** Medium
**Impact:** Critical

**Mitigation:**

- Offer free trial periods
- Launch incentives and early adopter benefits
- Demonstrate ROI with case studies
- Training and onboarding support
- Success stories from early adopters

**Contingency:**

- Adjust value proposition
- Add alternative monetization
- Direct broker recruitment team
- Partnership with broker networks

---

#### 2. **SLA Compliance Risk**

**Risk:** Brokers fail to meet 10-minute response SLA

**Likelihood:** High (initially)
**Impact:** High (undermines core promise)

**Mitigation:**

- Clear SLA expectations upfront
- Multiple notification channels (SMS + email + app)
- Gamification (badges, leaderboards)
- Penalties for repeated breaches (lose fast-track)
- Training on importance of speed

**Contingency:**

- Extend SLA to 15 minutes (if needed)
- Backup broker assignment (if primary doesn't respond)
- User expectations management
- Alternative broker pool

---

#### 3. **Property Quality Risk**

**Risk:** Low-quality or fake property listings

**Likelihood:** Medium
**Impact:** High (trust issues)

**Mitigation:**

- Admin verification required for fast-track
- Photo quality standards
- Broker verification process
- User reporting mechanism
- Regular audits

**Contingency:**

- Strict delisting policy
- Ban repeat offenders
- Enhanced verification (site visits)
- User review system

---

#### 4. **User Expectation Risk**

**Risk:** "24 hours" promise not met, users disappointed

**Likelihood:** Medium
**Impact:** High (reputation damage)

**Mitigation:**

- Clear explanation of what "24 hours" means
- Set realistic expectations (viewing in 24h, not ownership)
- Track and communicate progress
- Under-promise, over-deliver

**Contingency:**

- Adjust messaging to "fast-track process"
- Focus on "10-minute response" instead
- Transparent communication of delays

---

#### 5. **Platform Reliability Risk**

**Risk:** Downtime, bugs, lost leads

**Likelihood:** Low-Medium
**Impact:** Critical

**Mitigation:**

- Comprehensive testing before launch
- 24/7 monitoring
- Automated backups
- Redundancy in critical systems
- Incident response plan

**Contingency:**

- Manual lead assignment if automated fails
- Communication with affected users
- Service credits for affected brokers
- Rapid bug fixing process

---

#### 6. **Competition Risk**

**Risk:** Competitors (RIghtMove, MagicBricks) launch similar feature

**Likelihood:** Medium (after we prove model)
**Impact:** Medium

**Mitigation:**

- Move fast, establish brand
- Network effects (more brokers = more value)
- Superior execution
- Broker relationships and lock-in

**Contingency:**

- Accelerate feature development
- Focus on underserved markets (tier-2 cities)
- Differentiate on quality and speed
- Build strong broker loyalty
